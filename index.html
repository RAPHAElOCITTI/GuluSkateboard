Our Story

Founded on passion, driven by purpose

Building Community Through Skateboarding

Since 2013, Nelly Umar has been the driving force behind skateboarding culture in Gulu, Northern Uganda. What started as a personal passion became a community movement when GSC was formally established in 2021.

Northern Uganda faces unique challenges - the legacy of conflict, limited opportunities for youth, and socio-economic pressures that can lead vulnerable young people toward negative influences. We recognized skateboarding's power to transform lives and create positive change.

"Skateboarding isn't just a sport here - it's social cement. It rebuilds community, one push at a time."

Our Mission

To harness the transformative power of skateboarding to empower youth and children in Northern Uganda, providing positive alternatives, fostering community, building resilience, and steering them away from criminal activities and negative influences.

Our Vision

A thriving, empowered generation in Northern Uganda, united by skateboarding, equipped with life skills and actively shaping a brighter future for themselves and their community.

Programs & Projects

Creating pathways from the skatepark to the classroom and beyond

Free Skate Sessions

Regular skateboarding lessons and practice sessions open to all youth in the community. We provide boards, safety equipment, and expert instruction.

Learn to Skate Programs

Structured beginner programs focused on building confidence, basic skills, and safety awareness. Perfect for newcomers to skateboarding culture.

Community Events & Competitions

Regular competitions, jams, and community gatherings that showcase talent, build community spirit, and celebrate our skateboarding culture.

Youth Mentorship

Pairing experienced skaters with newcomers, developing leadership skills and creating positive role models within the community.

Future Skatepark Project

Working toward building Northern Uganda's first public skatepark - a dedicated, safe space for the community to grow and thrive.
