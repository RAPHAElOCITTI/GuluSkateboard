Our Story

More Than a Sport: Skateboarding as Social Transformation in Gulu

Founded by Nelly Umar in 2013, the Gulu Skateboard Community has grown from a small group of passionate skaters into a formally recognized organization transforming lives across Northern Uganda. What started as informal sessions has evolved into comprehensive programs that use skateboarding as a tool for youth empowerment, education, and community building.

Since our official launch in 2021, we've created safe spaces where young people can develop confidence, leadership skills, and resilience while pursuing their education and building brighter futures for themselves and their community.
